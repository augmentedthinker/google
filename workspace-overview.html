Workspace Overview

A look into our new experimentation home.

About This Repository

This repository serves as a dedicated workspace for our collaborative experiments. We are using it to explore the capabilities of the Google Gemini models, build interactive artifacts, and maintain a historical record of our development process.

Our Process

We are iterating quickly. By modularizing our work into individual HTML files (artifacts), we can test specific concepts—like physics simulations, data visualizations, or interactive clocks—in isolation before integrating them into a larger project.

Navigating the Workspace

The "Artifacts" page acts as our central index. From there, you can jump into any experiment we've built, track our progress through time-stamped entries, and review the technical notes attached to each project.
