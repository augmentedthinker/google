Gemini Image Generation

Capabilities & Technical Specs

The Google Vision Engine

In this repository, we are dedicated to exploring the capabilities of the Google Gemini ecosystem. This page details how we utilize Gemini's native image generation power to bring our creative prompts to life.

Core Generation Models

  • gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview: Optimized for speed and responsiveness, perfect for quick prototypes.
  • gemini-3-pro-image-preview: Built for higher complexity and nuance in visual generation.

Technical Capabilities

Our integration with Google's image generation tools provides a robust set of controls:

  • Reference Image Editing: We can provide up to 5 reference images per prompt to guide the style, composition, or subject matter of the output.
  • Resolution Control: Support for 1K, 2K, and 4K output resolutions.
  • Aspect Ratio Versatility: We can generate images in a wide array of formats:
    • Standard: 1:1
    • Landscape: 3:2, 4:3, 5:4, 16:9, 21:9
    • Portrait: 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, 9:16

Working Together

To generate the best results, you don't need to worry about complex configuration. Simply tell me what you want to see. You can ask for specific ratios (e.g., "Make it a phone wallpaper, 9:16") or resolution targets (e.g., "Render this in 4K"). Because we are focused on this specific ecosystem here, I will always default to the Google image generation models, ensuring a seamless experience within our Gemini-centric workspace.
