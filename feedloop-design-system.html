FeedLoop Design System

A scroll-optimized, content-first design system built for social media feeds.

Overview

FeedLoop emphasizes readability at speed, with compact spacing that maximizes content density without sacrificing clarity.

Do's and Don'ts

  • Do keep post cards visually uniform with consistent padding and card radius
  • Don't add heavy shadows to feed cards
  • Do maintain a minimum touch target of 44px for mobile interactions
